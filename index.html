FANGS: A MIGUEL O'HARA ZINE
x



FANGS is an upcoming for-profit zine centered around Across the Spiververse’s iteration of everyone’s favorite Spider Society leader, Miguel O’Hara! This zine will feature contributors’ interpretations and portrayals of Miguel O’Hara in any and all aspects, featuring him in art, writing, and even merch. The zine will be ship-free in its entirety and may also feature ATSV canon characters, so long as Miguel O’Hara is the main focus of the piece. The main zine is SFW, and we will be creating an NSFW side zine. Only 18+ individuals may purchase this zine.
