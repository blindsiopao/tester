THE TOWN OF NIGHT VALE
x

Night Vale is described as a small town in the desert, likley in the American Southwest. Outsides new to the town have frequently stated that it is hard to find, and while Cecil has alluded to leaving, it seems most of the townsfolk never do.

Night Vale is a bizarre little town, full of mishaps, monsters, and miracles. The librarians are vicious monsters, the diners serve invisible pie, and the waitresses and part tree. The secret police hide behind bushes and dogs are not allowed in the dog park.

Yet, however terrifying the ongoings may be, the town is full of charming people with a strong sense of community.


WELCOME TO NIGHT VALE
x

Welcome to Night Vale is a surreal fiction podcast set in desert town of Night Vale that has been airing twice a month since 2012. The podcast is told in the style of community radio updates from the station's host, Cecil Palmer.

While WTNV has many arcs that utilize long-form storytelling, most individual episodes can standalone as their own contained narratives.

Listeners will be begin recognizing recurring background characters, running jokes, and mysteries that are constantly alluded to.
