THE ANTAGONISTS
x

the game has three priamry "antagonists," difficult guests who take over the penthouse


FRANKIE FUSILLI: the mobster
x

frankie is the first antagonist of the game, and upon meeting him he demands free accomodations in the penthouse.

over the course of his stay he makes increasingly bizarre requests, such as building a vault in the basement, delivering an exploding cow head, and burying a wriggling chest in the desert.

however, when you go to bury the chest you are confronted by penelope the fbi agent, who confiscates the chest.

under pressure from the fbi, you wear a wire while conversing with frankie, and he admits that he's been laundering money since he arrived.

immediately after this he is taken away by penelope, but claims that he will be back in a few days.

he is provne correct when he returns later in the game as a regular hotel guest.

fun fact: after frankie leaves you can use his money press in the vault to print money, at the risk of being caught and fined


AVA CADAVRA: the cult leader
x

ava appears in town shortly after frankie fusilli's arrest, claiming the penthouse

she immediately has you create the bovine shrine in the basement, which contains a statue of the prime heifer for her cult to worship

soon she begins inducting people into her cult, and they can be seen trapped and chanting mindless at the shrine

the player then recieves a phonecall instructing them to go into the desert at night, where they locate an acient crypt and meet the mummy horus menhoset IX.

horus later instructs the player to retrieve his branding iron and use it on the prime heifer.

once the branding iron is used, the prime heifer is destroyed, causing ava cadavra to fly into a rage, light the penthouse on fire, and leave without payment


OPTIMUM ALFRED: the robot
x

a scheming robot, optimum alfred takes over the penthouse after ava's quick exit.

he first demands that you get him a galvatronic power recharger so that he can clean up the penthouse, then he starts building a satelite on the sun deck.

shortly after, optimum alfed announces his true intentions are to lead an robot invasion in strangetown in an attempt to take over.

once this happens, the player must act as the ratticator to defeat his sidekicks and team up with alien emperor xizzle to steal alfred's plans and eventually stop the invasion.

once optimum alfred is defeated, the main storyline is complete.


EMPEROR XIZZLE: the alien
x

emperor xizzle is a minor antagonist, an alien who is consistantly trying to invade strangetown.

he and his minions spawn randomly from time to time conducting minor invasions, but can be easily defeated once you spray them with water.

if you timeskip your ds while playing the game you will see an extended cutscene of a large scale alien invasion lead by emperor xizzle.

at the end of the game, the player teams up with him to take down optimum alfred.



THE RATTICATOR
x

the ratticator is a superhero that asissts the people of strangetown. the player takes on the identity of the ratticator after unlocking the rat cave.

as the ratticator, you are able to quickly take on goons, robots, and aliens.

fun fact: it is revealed that tristan legend was the original ratticator



THE SIMS 2 DS
x

sims 2 ds game was one of my favorite games growing up, even though it also scared the hell out of me. the game has an eerie, hotel california-like atmosphere that is extremely surreal and off-putting.

although the premise of the game was a simple hotel simulator, there were also a host of bizarre supblots, such as:

  • dealing with the mob
  • a cow cult in the basement
  • alien invasions
  • robot invasions
  • mummies
  • becoming a cat themed superhero
  • alien autopsies
  • discovering bigfoot

    • quite frankly, the game feels cursed



    THE PLOT
    x

    the sims 2 ds game begins with the player crashing their car in strangetown.

    jebidiah s. jerky suggests that you check into the local hotel, since your engine has "more holes than a donut shop."

    upon arrival the player learns that the manager of the hotel left the previous day, and that there's a note at the check-in desk addressed to the player's character, declaring them the new manager of the hotel.

    the object of the game is to maintain the hotel, improve the hotel by building rooms and buying objects, and to assist the electic group of guests with their daily requests.

    the game ends once you defeat the final antaognist, optimum alfred.



    ALTERING THE CLOCK
    x

    this game uses real-life time for many of it's mechanics, and is infamous for having skill poinst that are accessible only at bizarre times of day. because of this, many players may consider simply altering the clock on their ds.

    however, if you alter the clock while playing the sims 2, the game retaliates by calling you a "time-travelling cheater," and punishes you for it.


    upon re-entering the game, the player is treated to a cutscene of emperor xizzle leading an alien invasion in strangetown. additionally, the music is changed, it will now be pouring rain, and the player will only be able to move very, very slowly.

    honestly, the first time that this happened to me as a kid it freaked me out so much that i started an entirely new save file. it does alter your file draastically, i do not reccomend time skipping.

    THE SANITY METER
    x

    the sims 2 ds only has one meter for sims, called the sanity meter. you can raise your sanity by interacting with certain objects.

    sanity plumments quickly when venturing into the desert. if your sanity meter drops too low, you will black out and wake up in jail and have to pay bail to be released.

    players are also responsible for mainting the sanity of others sims, who repeastedly get agitated and loopy.



    ALIEN AUTOPSIES
    x

    alien autopsy is one of the mini games that you can play in the government lab.

    to play, players first use the metal detector to find a mummified alien in the desert.

    a good way to earn simoleons, gameplay involves carefully tracing healthy organs and adding them to the cooler.



    BIGFOOT
    x
